To Join Netflix App:

  1. Run SyftBox:
    curl -LsSf https://syftbox.openmined.org/install.sh | sh
  2. Install Netflix App:
    syftbox app install gubertoli/syftbox-netflix

Repository: https://github.com/gubertoli/syftbox-netflix

To get your viewing history: https://help.netflix.com/en/node/101917

Additional Steps:

  • Download your NetflixViewingHistory.csv from Netflix.
  • Update the OUTPUT_DIR in your .env file with the directory where your NetflixViewingHistory.csv is located.
